ABOUT

The Company Data's information is based on the original video game, Lethal Company.

Lethal Company is a co-op horror about scavenging at abandoned moons to sell scrap to the Company. The game features a voice chat system that is integral to the gameplay experience as it immerses the player along with others in completing quotas.

You are a contracted worker for the Company. Your job is to collect scrap from abandoned, industrialized moons to meet the Company's profit quota. You can use the cash you earn to travel to new moons with higher risks and rewards--or you can buy fancy suits and decorations for your ship.

To check out the game, be sure to visit the Lethal Company Steam page.

Detail of information are extracted from the Lethal Company Fandom wiki page.
